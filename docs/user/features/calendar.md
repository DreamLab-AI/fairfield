# Calendar

View upcoming events, plan gatherings, and coordinate with your community.

---

## Overview

The calendar helps your community stay organised:

- **View events** across your zones
- **RSVP** to let organisers know your plans
- **Create events** to bring people together
- **Set availability** so others know when you're free

### Switching Views

Click the view buttons in the calendar header to switch between Month, Week, Day, and List views.

## RSVPing to Events

Let organisers know your plans:

### RSVP Options

| Option | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Going** | You'll definitely attend |
| **Maybe** | You're interested but not committed |
| **Not Going** | You can't make it |

### How to RSVP

1. Open the event details
2. Click your response button
3. Your RSVP is recorded

### Changing Your RSVP

Click a different option to update your response. Organisers can see when responses change.

### Why RSVP Matters

- Helps organisers plan (catering, space, etc.)
- Shows others the event has interest
- Gets you reminders as the event approaches
- Lets people know you'll be there

## Creating Events

### Who Can Create Events

This depends on your zone and permissions. Typically:
- All members can create events in some channels
- Some sections require organiser permissions
- Administrators can create events anywhere

### Create an Event

1. Click **Create Event** in the calendar
2. Fill in the details:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Title** | Clear, descriptive name | Yes |
| **Date & Time** | When it starts | Yes |
| **End Time** | When it finishes | Recommended |
| **Location** | Physical address or online link | Recommended |
| **Description** | All the details people need | Recommended |
| **Zone/Channel** | Where to announce it | Yes |

3. Click **Create** to publish

### Event Tips

**Good event titles:**
- "January Social Meetup" âœ…
- "Meeting" âŒ (too vague)
- "SUPER IMPORTANT EVENT!!!" âŒ (too much)

**Good descriptions include:**
- What will happen
- What to bring (if anything)
- How to find the venue
- Contact info for questions
- Accessibility information

## Availability (DreamLab & Family)

Some zones use availability tracking:

### Setting Your Availability

1. Go to the calendar
2. Click on a time slot
3. Select your status:
   - **Available** â€” Free for meetings/coordination
   - **Busy** â€” Working but not free for new things
   - **Away** â€” Not available (holiday, personal)

- Find times that work for everyone

## Reminders

### Getting Reminded

You'll receive reminders for events you're attending:
- Notification before the event starts
- Reminder in your feed

### Reminder Timing

Default reminders typically come:
- 1 day before
- 1 hour before

Adjust timing in your notification settings.

---

## Cross-Zone Calendar

If you're in multiple zones:

### What You See

- Your personal calendar shows events from all your zones
- Events are colour-coded or labelled by zone
- You can filter to show only certain zones

### Privacy

- Event details stay within their zone
- You can see your own events across zones
- Others only see events in zones they belong to

## Troubleshooting

<details>
<summary><strong>I can't create an event</strong></summary>

Check these things:
- Do you have permission in that zone/channel?
- Is event creation enabled for members?
- Contact an administrator if you need permissions

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>An event isn't showing on my calendar</strong></summary>

Try these steps:
1. Check your zone filter settings
2. Make sure you're viewing the right date range
3. Refresh the calendar
4. Check if the event was cancelled

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>I RSVPed but the organiser didn't see it</strong></summary>

Your RSVP should appear immediately. If not:
- Check your internet connection
- Try RSVPing again
- Contact the organiser directly

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How do I cancel an event I created?</strong></summary>

1. Open the event details
2. Click **Edit** (if you're the organiser)
3. Select **Cancel Event** or **Delete**
4. Attendees will be notified

</details>

# Reactions

Express yourself with emoji reactions on messages.

---

## What Are Reactions?

Reactions are emoji responses you can add to messages. They're a quick way to:

- **Acknowledge** you've seen something
- **Agree** without adding a message
- **Express** emotion
- **Vote** informally
- **Celebrate** good news

---

## Adding Reactions

### Quick React

1. Hover over a message
2. Click the **emoji icon** that appears
3. Select an emoji from the picker
4. Your reaction is added

### Keyboard Shortcut

Some frequently used reactions have shortcuts:
- Hover over the message
- Press the corresponding key (if shown)

## Viewing Reactions

### On Messages

Reactions appear below the message:
- Each unique emoji shows once
- The count shows how many people reacted with it
- Click a reaction to see who reacted

### Who Reacted

To see the full list:
1. Click on the reaction emoji
2. A list of names appears
3. See everyone who used that reaction

---

## Managing Your Reactions

### Removing Your Reaction

Click the same emoji again to remove your reaction.

### Changing Your Reaction

1. Remove your current reaction (click it)
2. Add a different reaction

### Multiple Reactions

You can react with multiple different emojis to the same message.

## Reaction Etiquette

### When to Use Reactions

âœ… **Good uses:**
- Quick acknowledgement
- Expressing agreement
- Celebrating announcements
- Informal polling
- Reducing message clutter

âŒ **Less appropriate:**
- When a reply is needed
- Passive-aggressive responses
- Reaction spam
- Important feedback (use words instead)

### Reactions vs Replies

| Use Reactions When | Use Replies When |
|--------------------|------------------|
| A simple acknowledgement is enough | You have something to add |
| Many people feel the same | Your response is unique |
| You don't want to notify everyone | The person needs to see your response |
| It's casual/social | It's important/actionable |

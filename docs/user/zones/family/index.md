## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I add someone to the Family zone?</strong></summary>

Family zone membership is carefully managed. Speak with the family administrator about adding new members.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How secure are the documents I store here?</strong></summary>

Documents are stored with the same security as the rest of the platform. For highly sensitive documents (like financial records with account numbers), consider what level of detail you need to store.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can other family members see my private messages?</strong></summary>

No. Private messages are encrypted end-to-end. Only you and the person you're messaging can read them.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What happens if I accidentally share something to the wrong channel?</strong></summary>

You can delete your own messages. If you've shared something sensitive, delete it and let affected family members know.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How do I get notified about important updates?</strong></summary>

You can configure notifications in your settings. We recommend enabling notifications for the Announcements channel at minimum.

</details>

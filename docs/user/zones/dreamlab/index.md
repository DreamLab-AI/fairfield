# DreamLab

![DreamLab Hero](../../../../static/images/zones/dreamlab-hero.webp)

**Creative technology community for Manchester, Cumbria, and the North West of England.**

---

## Welcome to DreamLab

DreamLab is where business meets creativity, technology meets community. This space supports the embedded business and brings together developers, designers, makers, and creative technologists across the North West of England.

---

Beyond business, DreamLab fosters a creative community:

## Getting Started in DreamLab

### If You're Here for Business

1. **Check the Operations channel** for current priorities
2. **Review the Calendar** for upcoming meetings and deadlines
3. **Update your availability** so colleagues know when you're free
4. **Join project channels** relevant to your work

### If You're Part of the Community

1. **Introduce yourself** in the General channel
2. **Share your skills and interests** so others can connect
3. **Browse the Projects channel** to see what people are building
4. **Join regional discussions** for your area

### Creating Events

To schedule something:

1. Navigate to the **Calendar**
2. Click **Create Event**
3. Fill in details:
   - Title and description
   - Date and time
   - Duration
   - Participants (optional)
4. Choose which channel to notify

## Privacy & Confidentiality

### Business Confidentiality

DreamLab takes business privacy seriously:

- Business channels are only visible to authorised team members
- Client information stays within project channels
- Private messages are fully encrypted
- Access is controlled and auditable

### Community Privacy

For community members:

- Your profile shows only what you choose to share
- Private messages are encrypted end-to-end
- You control your own visibility

### Cross-Zone Separation

DreamLab is separate from other zones:

- Minimoonoir members cannot see DreamLab content unless they're also DreamLab members
- Family zone content is completely separate
- Calendar can show personal availability across zones, but event details stay within each zone

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary><strong>How do I join DreamLab?</strong></summary>

DreamLab access is managed based on your relationship with the business or community. Contact the team if you believe you should have access.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I share DreamLab content externally?</strong></summary>

Community content is generally shareable. Business content should be treated as confidential unless explicitly marked for external sharing.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How do I create a project channel?</strong></summary>

Project channels are created by administrators. Discuss your needs in the Operations channel, and the team can set up appropriate spaces.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What if I'm not in Manchester, Cumbria, or the North West?</strong></summary>

The community welcomes anyone interested in creative technology, regardless of location. The regional focus helps with local events and meetups, but remote participation is valued.

</details>

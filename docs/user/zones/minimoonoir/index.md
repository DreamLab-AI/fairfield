## Getting Involved

### Joining Conversations

1. Browse the available channels in the sidebar
2. Click a channel to view its messages
3. Type in the message box to contribute
4. Use reactions to respond without adding noise

### Organising Events

Ready to bring people together?

1. Go to the **Calendar** section
2. Click **Create Event**
3. Fill in the details:
   - Event name and description
   - Date, time, and duration
   - Location (physical or virtual)
   - Which channel to announce in
4. Share with the community

### Building Connections

Tips for getting the most from Minimoonoir:

## Privacy in Minimoonoir

### What's Public Within the Zone

- Messages in channels are visible to all zone members
- Your profile information is visible to members
- Event attendance (RSVPs) is visible to organisers

### What's Private

- Your private messages are encrypted and unreadable by anyone else
- Your exact location is never shared automatically
- Your contact details are only shared if you choose

### Cross-Zone Visibility

Minimoonoir is separate from other zones:

- DreamLab members cannot see Minimoonoir channels unless they're also Minimoonoir members
- Family members cannot see Minimoonoir content
- The calendar can show events across zones you're part of, but details remain within each zone

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary><strong>How do I join Minimoonoir?</strong></summary>

Minimoonoir requires an invitation from an existing member or approval from a community administrator. If you know someone in the community, ask them for an introduction.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I organise events in any location?</strong></summary>

Absolutely! Minimoonoir welcomes events anywhere in the UK. Regional channels help you find people in your area, but events can be organised anywhere.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What if I can't attend an event I RSVPed to?</strong></summary>

Update your RSVP as soon as you know. Event organisers appreciate knowing the numbers, especially for events that require booking or planning.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How do I suggest a new channel or section?</strong></summary>

Discuss your idea in the General channel. If there's community interest, administrators can create new spaces.

</details>

# Safety & Privacy

Learn how your data is protected and how to stay safe on the platform.

---

## Overview

Your safety and privacy are built into the platform's design:

- **End-to-end encryption** for private messages
- **Decentralised architecture** — no single point of control
- **Your keys, your data** — cryptographic ownership
- **Privacy by design** — minimal data collection

## Quick Safety Tips

### Protect Your Account

✅ **Do:**
- Store your recovery phrase securely offline
- Use a unique passphrase if offered
- Review connected sessions periodically
- Log out on shared devices

❌ **Don't:**
- Share your recovery phrase with anyone
- Enter your recovery phrase on suspicious sites
- Click links in unexpected messages
- Use public computers for sensitive actions

### Privacy Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Review privacy settings when you join
- Be mindful of what you share publicly
- Use private messages for sensitive conversations
- Report suspicious behaviour

❌ **Don't:**
- Share personal details in public channels
- Assume all messages are private (check the channel type)
- Accept unexpected requests without verification
- Ignore your instincts about suspicious activity

## Security Features

### What's Protected

| Feature | Protection Level |
|---------|-----------------|
| Private messages | End-to-end encrypted |
| Channel messages | Transport encrypted |
| Your profile | Cryptographically signed |
| Your identity | Key-based, pseudonymous |
| Your data | Decentralised storage |

### What This Means

- **End-to-end encrypted** — Only you and the recipient can read
- **Transport encrypted** — Protected while moving across the internet
- **Cryptographically signed** — Cannot be forged or tampered with
- **Key-based** — Your identity is a cryptographic key, not a username/password
- **Decentralised** — No single company controls all data
